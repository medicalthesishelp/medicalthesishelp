About the Service

This service was established to support investigator-initiated medical research by assisting clinicians and researchers in converting clinical ideas and datasets into structured academic outputs suitable for:

Institutional Ethics Committees (IEC) University thesis evaluation and viva examinations Peer-reviewed indexed journals

Support is provided across all stages of research documentation while ensuring that ownership of data, conclusions, authorship, and submission decisions remains entirely with the investigator.

All work is prepared with the expectation of rigorous academic, ethical, and editorial scrutiny.

Comprehensive Services

Services are comprehensive, modular, and flexible, and may be engaged independently or across the complete research continuum.

Research Protocol Development

We provide end-to-end protocol development across all levels of the evidence-based research pyramid.

Protocols are prepared for:
  • Case reports & Case series
  • Cross-sectional studies & Case–control studies
  • Cohort studies
  • Diagnostic accuracy studies
  • Interventional and comparative studies
  • Clinical audits and quality-improvement projects
  • Systematic reviews and meta-analyses
Protocol components include:
  • Scientific background and literature-based rationale
  • Clearly defined aims, objectives, and hypotheses
  • Study design and detailed methodology
  • Study setting and population
  • Inclusion and exclusion criteria
  • ICD-based disease classification and coding
  • Case Record Form (CRF) structure
  • Outcome measures and operational definitions
  • Statistical methodology and analysis plan
  • Ethical considerations and regulatory compliance
Additional protocol services:
  • Local-language translation of: Patient Information Sheets, Informed Consent Forms, Assent forms
  • Alignment with ICMR and NDCTR requirements
  • Documentation prepared for IEC submission and approval
Statistical Planning & Sample Size Determination

Structured statistical planning is provided to ensure methodological validity and transparency.

Includes:
  • Sample size calculation
  • Statistical power analysis
  • Effect size estimation
  • Documentation of assumptions
  • Selection and justification of statistical tests
  • Development of a Statistical Analysis Plan (SAP)

Prepared for inclusion in protocols, theses, and journal manuscripts.

Statistical Analysis & Results Preparation

We provide complete statistical analysis support, from basic descriptive methods to advanced modelling. All analysis uses recent versions of apps including SPSS, R, JAMOVI, etc.

Descriptive statistics:
  • Mean, median, standard deviation
  • Interquartile range
  • Frequencies and percentages
Inferential statistics:
  • Chi-square test & Fisher’s exact test
  • Student’s t-test & Mann–Whitney U test
  • ANOVA & Kruskal–Wallis test
  • Correlation analyses
Advanced analyses:
  • Logistic regression & Linear and multivariable regression
  • ROC curve analysis
  • Survival analysis (Kaplan–Meier curves, Hazard ratios with confidence intervals)
Deliverables include:
  • Cleaned and coded datasets
  • Tables and figures suitable for theses and journals
  • Clear academic interpretation of results
  • Statistical summaries ready for manuscripts
