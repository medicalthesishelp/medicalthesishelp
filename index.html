We provide comprehensive medical writing, research documentation, statistical analysis, and publication support for clinicians, postgraduate trainees, and academic researchers.

About

This service was established to support investigator-initiated medical research by assisting clinicians and researchers in converting clinical ideas and datasets into structured academic outputs suitable for:

Institutional Ethics Committees (IEC) University thesis & viva Peer-reviewed indexed journals

Support is provided across all stages of research documentation while ensuring that ownership of data, conclusions, authorship, and submission decisions remains entirely with the investigator.

All work is prepared with the expectation of rigorous academic, ethical, and editorial scrutiny.

Services

Services are comprehensive, modular, and flexible, and may be engaged independently or across the complete research continuum.

Research Protocol Development

We provide end-to-end protocol development across all levels of the evidence-based research pyramid.

Protocols are prepared for:

  • Case reports & Case series
  • Cross-sectional & Case–control studies
  • Cohort & Diagnostic accuracy studies
  • Interventional & comparative studies
  • Clinical audits & quality-improvement projects
  • Systematic reviews & meta-analyses

Protocol components include:

  • Scientific background and literature-based rationale
  • Clearly defined aims, objectives, and hypotheses
  • Study design and detailed methodology
  • Study setting, population, inclusion/exclusion criteria
  • ICD-based disease classification and coding
  • Case Record Form (CRF) structure
  • Outcome measures and operational definitions
  • Statistical methodology and analysis plan
Statistical Planning & Sample Size Determination

Structured statistical planning is provided to ensure methodological validity and transparency.

  • Sample size calculation & Statistical power analysis
  • Effect size estimation & Documentation of assumptions
  • Selection and justification of statistical tests
  • Development of a Statistical Analysis Plan (SAP)
Statistical Analysis & Results Preparation

We provide complete statistical analysis support, from basic descriptive methods to advanced modelling using recent versions of SPSS, R, and JAMOVI.

  • Descriptive: Mean, median, standard deviation, interquartile range, frequencies.
  • Inferential: Chi-square test, Fisher's exact test, Student's t-test, Mann–Whitney U test, ANOVA, Kruskal–Wallis test, correlation analyses.
  • Advanced: Logistic/linear regression, ROC curve analysis, survival analysis, Kaplan–Meier curves, hazard ratios.
Thesis & Dissertation Support

Complete academic documentation support for MD | MS | MCh | DNB | PhD | MDS | BDS.

Covers all thesis sections: Introduction, Literature review, Aims and objectives, Methodology, Statistical analysis, Results, Discussion, Conclusion and limitations, References and annexures.

Additional support: University-specific formatting, reference structuring, plagiarism screening, AI-content detection review, language editing and submission readiness.

Manuscript Writing & Journal Publication

Full publication support, extending beyond manuscript drafting.

  • Manuscript preparation from original datasets or thesis material
  • Guidance on journal selection (indexed journals only)
  • Journal-specific formatting and reference management
  • Online manuscript submission on journal portals
  • Detailed point-by-point responses to reviewer comments
  • Revised manuscript preparation and resubmission
Literature Reviews & Evidence Synthesis

Support for all forms of scholarly reviews: narrative reviews, systematic reviews, meta-analyses, scoping reviews.

  • Research question formulation (PICO / PECO)
  • Literature search strategy development
  • Study selection, data extraction, and risk-of-bias assessment
  • Evidence synthesis and PRISMA-aligned reporting
Scientific & Academic Presentations

Preparation of structured academic material for professional and academic settings.

  • Scientific posters & Oral presentation slide decks
  • Journal Club (JC) presentations & Critical appraisal
  • Data visualisation and graphical representation
Pharmacovigilance & Safety Documentation

Documentation support in drug and device safety.

  • Adverse Drug Reaction (ADR) documentation
  • Case narratives & Periodic safety update reports
  • Cosmetovigilance & Materiovigilance documentation

Experience

  • Extensive work on postgraduate thesis projects within teaching hospitals, dealing with real-world constraints such as fixed timelines, limited sample sizes, incomplete records, and retrospective data.
